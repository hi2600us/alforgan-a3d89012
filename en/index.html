The project

Alforgan Mixed-Use Complex

Four components working together: two generate sustainable waqf income, and two carry the direct spiritual and educational impact — in Afra, Balqarn Governorate, Asir Region.

Revenue-generating

Hospitality Building

Hotel suites and rooms serving regional visitors, forming the main income source that funds the endowment after operation begins.

Spiritual & educational

Educational Nursery

A nursery dedicated to grounding young children in the Holy Quran in a safe environment, in partnership with the Balqarn Quran Memorization Society.

Revenue-generating

Café & Reception

Community spaces serving the local area, growing the endowment's revenue alongside the hospitality building.

Spiritual & educational

Alforgan Mosque

Ongoing funding for the maintenance and upkeep of the mosque located directly east of the building.

About the waqf

Alforgan Charitable Endowment & Women's Quran Memorization House

This blessed edifice was founded by the grace of God, and then through the vision and generous hand of Sheikh Abdullah bin Saad bin Hanash Al-Qarni (may God have mercy on him), who embodied the highest meanings of giving and benevolence when he endowed this building. He left behind an inexhaustible legacy for the community and a journey of goodness whose rewards extend to the Day of Judgment — a model to be followed in relieving hardship and loving good for society.

Beneficiary

Investment of the building for the benefit of the Balqarn Quran Memorization Charitable Society, and to spend on the needs of Alforgan Mosque located east of the building.

A self-sustaining endowment

One contribution · lasting legacy

The endowment is in its final development phase. Once completed, the building's income will perpetually fund Quran memorization and mosque care on its own — no further donations needed. Your partnership today plants a seed that gives, generation after generation.

Frequently asked questions

Questions people ask us

What is Alforgan Charitable Endowment?

Alforgan Charitable Endowment & Women's Quran Memorization House is a registered charitable waqf (Registration ID 1112506318) in Afra, Balqarn Governorate, Asir Region, Saudi Arabia. It invests in a mixed-use building — a hotel, a Quran memorization nursery, a café, and a mosque — directing its income to the Balqarn Quran Memorization Society and Alforgan Mosque.

What does "one-time donation" mean here?

Once finishing works are complete and the building is operating, commercial income from the hotel and café will permanently fund Quran memorization and mosque upkeep on its own, with no future donations needed. Your contribution today is a single investment in an ongoing, self-renewing charity.

What phase is the project in right now?

The project is in Phase 2 of 4: the concrete structure is complete, and interior and exterior finishing works are underway. Remaining work is expected to take about one year, God willing.

How can I donate to Alforgan Endowment?

By direct bank transfer to the endowment's account at Al Rajhi Bank. To confirm your transfer and receive a receipt, please contact the trustee board at admin@alforgan.org or +966 50 560 9022.

Where is the Alforgan project located?

The project is located in Afra, Balqarn Governorate, Asir Region, Saudi Arabia, directly beside Alforgan Mosque.

How do I register interest in the Quran memorization circles?

Through the "Quran circles" registration page on the endowment's website. You will be contacted once registration formally opens after the building is completed.
